1. Jury Specifications

     1.1 What is the SEER DAO Jury

             SEER DAO is a decentralized autonomous organization of SEER. The holders of SEER-DSN have SEER DAO governance rights over the SEER protocol, including voting rights, community proposal rights, and improving community governance efficiency. Anyone can become a holder of SEER -DSN and participate in the future construction of SEER DAO.

 

The SEER DAO jury is the upper-level autonomous organization of SEER DAO and an important part of SEER's decentralized autonomy. The members of the jury will be selected from the SEER -DSN community and become DAO reviewers. They can vote and review proposals, activities and projects initiated by the community. The approved projects or proposals can be incorporated into SEER's future development plan for implementation, and more important thing is that the activities in AOE and ADS can be voted and reviewed. Of course, there are corresponding rewards and rewards to realize the governance structure of the community of interests.

 

1.2 How to join the SEER DAO Jury

             Please note: If you want to join the SEER DAO jury, you need to apply to become a DAO member first. The application conditions for becoming a DAO member are as follows:

● DSN held for more than 30 days

● Verified by Twitter

● Familiar with SEER DAO related specifications

● Pass the assessment rules ability test (requires 80 points)

Successfully meeting the above conditions can become a member of SEER DAO.

 

1.3 SEER Jury Review Rules

             1.3.1 How to exercise rights after becoming a jury. Reviewers need to refer to the "SEER User Agreement", "SEER User Privacy Agreement" and "SEER Community Space Guidelines", and conduct fair and just voting according to the actual situation of the proposal, the system will automatically record the voting results in real time; Make your own judgment and choose the "Pass" or "Reject" option;

1.3.2 Voting rules and application

AOE/ADS proposal will take effect when the following conditions are met:

● The number of valid votes reaches more than 60% .

● The voting time meets the set time of 12 hours.

If the voted proposal passes the review, then the proposal will be submitted by the SEER DAO jury to the SEER platform and automatically listed on the AOE or ADS curation window. If the voted proposal fails the review, the proposal will fail because it is Global DAO reviewers participate in the review and you will not receive a reason for rejection .

The voter turnout rate of curators is an important weight that affects the display order of curatorial activities. Please cast your key vote properly and carefully.

1.4 Rules of Conduct for SEER DAO Jury

            

             The Jury's Commitment

As members, contributors and leaders, we are committed to empowering everyone involved in our community to truly exercise their rights. No matter where you come from in the world, no matter your expression, level of experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, caste, color, religion, etc., we are committed to helping to build an open, positive, diverse, inclusive and healthy community ways to act and collaborate with each other.

 

            Standards of Conduct for the Jury

           Examples of behaviors that create a positive environment for our community include:

● Strictly abide by the relevant rules of the "SEER User Agreement", "SEER User Privacy Agreement" and "SEER Community Space Guidelines" for review, and vote fairly and justly according to the case environment.

● Show empathy and kindness to others.

● Respect different opinions, viewpoints and experiences.

● Give and receive constructive feedback gracefully.

● Take responsibility and learn from experience.

● Focus on what is best, not only for us as individuals, but for the community as a whole.

● Adhere to an objective, neutral and rigorous evaluation attitude, and try to avoid affecting the evaluation results due to personal position, likes and dislikes.

● Be responsible for the review results and do not vote blindly.

● Do not manipulate or control the review results in any way, interfere with the review order, and strictly prohibit all forms of malicious vote brushing.

● SEER DAO reviewers need to lead by example and maintain a fair and just environment for the review platform.

 

            The evaluator will be disqualified if any of the following occurs during his tenure:

 

SEER Community Space Guidelines during tenure.

DAO Jury Norms during the tenure.

SEER User Agreement during tenure.

● Attempting to steal members' private information during their tenure.

● Behaviors such as extortion and vote cheating that damage the image and interests of the platform's DAO jury, etc.

● Use pornographic language or images, as well as any form of language to attack other DAO members in the community.

● Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks.

● Public or private harassment.

● Post his private information without the express permission of others.

● Other conduct that might reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

 

  1.5 Other dependencies

According to the different stages of community development, SEER DAO continuously improves this specification for the purpose of maintaining community order. Once the content of this specification changes, SEER DAO will directly publish the revised specification online. Please pay attention to relevant updates or revisions. Notice. If you continue to participate in the review activities, you agree to accept the revised content. If you have any objection to the revised specification, please stop the review activities immediately.
