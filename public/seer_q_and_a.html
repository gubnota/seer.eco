10. What is AOE?

AOE (Area Of Effect) is a paid advertising and marketing tool tailored for brand owners. It plays an important role in SEER ecology. It helps brands upgrade through public domain curation and introduce targeted users to the community. AOE selects and gathers valuable Web3 users in the public domain through blockchain data algorithm, which can greatly help brands increase their exposure and capture and retain more users through their marketing activities. It has become an indispensable value marketing tool, a must for all SEER brands.

11. What is ADS?

ADS is an important advertising and marketing protocol of SEER. Like AOE, it is an advertising and marketing tool tailored by SEER for brands. It offers precise positioning, flexible advertisement and high conversion rates. Therefore, it is one of the favorite advertising tools for brand owners. ADS focuses on tier 1 Web3 users, so it can bring precise and high-value Web3 users to brand owners. ADS provides brand owners with users that precisely match their user portraits. Through such precise curation of ADS, brands can further expand their influence.

12. What is the DAO proposal?

To build a fair and reasonable community, the governance of SEER DAO is automatically executed by smart contracts. In SEER DAO, decisions are made through voting on community proposals, which include technology upgrades, product innovation, market operation, token governance, etc. Each proposal is key to the future development of the community. If a proposal is voted by the jury, it will be incorporated into the future development plan for implementation.

13. What is gating?

Gating is an important function of SEER for community space governance. It can help the Web3 community to conduct hierarchical governance and preserve the unity of community users. By setting different gating strategies, it can effectively control and select the liquidity behavior of community members. For example; if the community is a BAYC community, it will assign users to different rooms through their equity ratings. The function of gating is to accurately select and manage target users by setting thresholds. If you are a BAYC holder, then you will be able to enter the room, or if you were a BAYC holder, you can also enter the room. Otherwise, you cannot enter. The specific policy criteria vary according to the needs of the space creator.

14. What is the treasury

The treasury is a financial strategy formulated by SEER for the development of the global Web3 community and DAO organizations. The current financial situation of the global Web3 community is very well. To facilitate effective fund management by the DAO community, SEER supports different organizations within the DAO by adding treasuries. It provides a great way of on-chain fund review, usage, supervision, and KYC compliance. SEER supports the establishment and permission setting of multiple treasuries, and supports monthly billing.

15. What is DAO management?

SEER DAO refers to the decentralized autonomous organization of SEER. SEER's dynamic DSN holders have governance rights over SEER DAO, including voting rights, proposal rights, and other rights to improve community governance efficiency. Anyone can become the holder of the dynamic SEER DSN and participate in the future management and construction of SEER. The SEER DAO jury plays an important role in it, and can review the proposals of the community, especially the voting review of AOE and ADS activities.
